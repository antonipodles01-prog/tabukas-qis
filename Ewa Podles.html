Biography

I was born in 1975 in Poland. In 2002 I graduated from Wrocław Academy of Fine Arts in glass and ceramics. In 2002 I received my diploma in glass design as well as my diploma in painting. At the moment I am living and creating in the United Kingdom.

My works are collected in Poland, Sweden, Switzerland, Canada, Germany, Italy, the United Arab Emirates and the United Kingdom. I use acrylic, oil and gilding on wooden panels. Recently I moved on to create digital paintings.
