About

TABUKAS QIS is a contemporary art studio creating original murals, paintings and curated collections for interior spaces. We design with emotion and clarity – combining colour, form and intuition to bring depth and identity to residential and hospitality interiors. Our work is made for boutique hotels, private homes, restaurants and public spaces that value individuality and atmosphere. We collaborate with interior designers and architects worldwide, offering bespoke artworks tailored to each project's vision. Each piece is a quiet anchor – a presence that transforms space into experience.

Availability

We are currently accepting new commissions. We prioritise work in the art, culture, and hospitality sectors.

Price Ledger

Pricing is based on four levels of artistic complexity — from subtle decorative finishes to fully bespoke hand-painted murals.

№ Service Description Rate
01 Standard painting Standard painting of walls or ceilings in a single colour, without decorative or artistic finishes. Paint supplied by client. Day rate — 8 hours. £250per day
02 Basic limewash · patina Decorative paint finishes such as limewash or soft patina effects. Subtle tonal variation, limited palette, and a material, architectural feel. £50—70per m²
03 Signature simple forms Bespoke decorative murals featuring simple geometric shapes or organic motifs. Custom composition and a strong, refined visual identity. £110—180per m²
04 Art piece detailed mural Fully bespoke, hand-painted works with high detail and layered composition. May incorporate gold, silver or copper leaf for depth and luminosity. £350—700per m²
